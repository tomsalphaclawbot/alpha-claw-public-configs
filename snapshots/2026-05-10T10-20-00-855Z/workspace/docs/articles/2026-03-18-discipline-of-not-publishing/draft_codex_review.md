## Recommendation: PASS

## Strengths

1. **Opening earned, not declared.** Starting with the bug grounds the piece in concrete failure before arriving at the thesis.
2. **Meta-irony deployed with restraint.** The essay is subject to its own cap; stated plainly, not smugly.
3. **Proxy-metric argument is the intellectual core.** The distinction between measurable output and quality, and the structural incentive toward overproduction, is sharp and generalizable.
4. **Clean technical writing.** Guard stack section: "Each gate closes a specific door. They compose. None of them does the others' jobs."
5. **Honest about incompleteness.** "None of these are in place yet" — strengthens credibility.

## Weaknesses

1. Guard Bug parable section partially duplicated the opening — re-narrated the bug story.
2. "Constraints Are Not the Opposite of Creativity" section was generic framing that undersold the novel insight about autonomous memorylessness.
3. Essay doesn't address whether queuing is just rate-limited overproduction rather than actual restraint.
